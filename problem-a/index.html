Harvey Specter - The Best Closer In The City

Gabriel Macht plays Harvey Specter in the TV Series "Suits", who is a legendary corporate lawyer or some people say he is the best closer in the city. He almost never loses, and he worked very hard from an assistant district attorney to the managing partner of Specter Litt, which is one of the best law firms in New York. He is righteous and smart, he is also emotional and caring for other people. He is a complexity of many things, a very intersting character to watch.

  • He is logical and can see through people.
  • Even though his work is busy, he finds time to have fun in life.
  • He values loyalty and will do anything for the poeple he loves.
